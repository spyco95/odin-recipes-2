Buffalo Chicken Dip

This tangy, creamy Buffalo chicken dip tastes just like Buffalo chicken wings! Perfect for parties and game dat, this chicken dip is best served hot with crackers and celery sticks. Everyone loves the results!

Ingredients

  • Chicken: This Buffalo chicken dip starts with two 10oz cans of chunck chicken, making it extra easy and affordable. Of course, you can substiture rotisserie chicken or shredded chicken breasts if you'd prefer.
  • Cream Cheese: Make sure the cream cheese (16oz) is softened for easy incorporation.
  • Ranch Dressing: A cup of ranch dressing adds creaminess and tang.
  • Hot Sauce: Hot pepper sauce (such as Frank's) turns up the heat, mimicking the spicy flavor of Buffalo chicken wings. Use more or less than the recipe calls for (3/4 cup) to suit your flavor preferences.
  • Cheese: Cheddar cheese (1 1/2 cups) is melted into the dip, creating a satisfyingly gooey texture and cheesy flavor.

Steps

  1. Heat chicken and hot pepper sauce in a skillet over medium heat until heated through, 3 to 5 minutes. Stir in cream cheese and ranch dressing. Cook and stir until well blended and warm, 3 to 5 minutes.
  2. Mix in 1/2 of the Cheddar cheese, and transfer the mixture to a slow cooker. Sprinkle the remaining cheese over the top.
  3. Cover and cook on Low until dip is hot and bubbly, about 35 minutes.
  4. Serve with celery sticks and crackers.
